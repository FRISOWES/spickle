About Spickle

Spickle B.V. was founded in 2025 by Friso Wesseling as a boutique consulting firm focused on technology and innovation. We provide hands-on leadership and strategic guidance to help companies navigate growth and change.

Friso is an experienced leader with a track record of driving success in high-growth ventures. He held leadership roles at Daraz (Alibaba Group’s e-commerce platform), co-founded and served as COO of fintech startup Koko, and led initiatives at Vertical AI. This background gives Spickle unique insight into scaling startups and corporate innovation.
